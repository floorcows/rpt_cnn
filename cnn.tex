\section{Convolutional Neural Network}

The CNN is trained on the CIFAR-10 dataset. It is a 60000 images dataset containing 32x32 colored and labeled images. There are 10 labels:

\begin{table}[H]
  \centering
  \begin{tabular}{|lllll|}
    \hline Airplane & Automobile & Bird  & Cat  & Deer  \\
    Dog      & Frog       & Horse & Ship & Truck \\
    \hline
  \end{tabular}
  \caption{CIFAR-10 classes}
\end{table}

To be able to correctly classify the input images, a multi layer CNN is implemented using the provided coefficient files.
